Other than drawing and reading, I also like to play instruments. If I need to
recommend someone to learn instrument, I will ask them to learn playing
kalimba because it is beginner friendly. Kalimba also helps me to relax and
de-stress. It also soothes my mind and calms me with its beautiful melodies and
calming sounds. Sometimes, I'm creating my own kalimba tabs from a piano sheet
because I cannot find any music cover that uses Kalimba on YouTube. I also learn
to play kalimba so I can cover my favourite song. In the future, I hope I can cover
as many songs as possible and teach others to play kalimba to make them feel that
learning instruments is easy and fun.
