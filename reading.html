During my free time, I will spend my time reading. In my opinion, books are like
a portal to me to travel across the world that I want to go to. Out of all the
books that I have read, one of my favourite book is Koc 13 by Naim Tamdjis.
The novel tells the story of Raiqal, a mysterious traveller and Aiman ​​Aklaken, an
international bestselling horror writer who travel to the realm of fantasy by boarding
Coach 13 that appears once every four years at an Dabong Railway Station. In the adventure,
they have met Raihan who has been trapped in that world for 4 years. Together, they need to
work as a team to get out of that world and complete all the tasks given at each stop. They
had to perform tasks in the cemetery, save themselves from zombies, fight robots from the future,
and other yet ridiculous tasks.

In my opinion, the storytelling of this novel is quite relaxed and the insertion of funny scenes
liven up the movement of the story. That is indeed the advantage of the writer, Naim Tamdjis since
he wisely controls the story plot. For the ending, although maybe not as smooth as the train stops,
but it leave a lot of feeling to the reader.
