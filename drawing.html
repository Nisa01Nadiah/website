I started painting as a hobby when I was a little.
I didn't know I had any talent. I believe talent is just a pursued interest.
Anybody can do what I do.
~ Bob Ross

One of my favourite hobbies is drawing. With drawing, I can express my
feelings and paint everything that I want on my canvas. Drawing also is
my therapy and makes me feel more creative since I have no rules on how to
create art. When drawing, I will find inspiration from my surroundings.
I also find inspiration from Pinterest, Instagram and TikTok. To create a
new art, I will take a few hours to a month to finish it. Even though it is
not pretty in my eyes, at least I feel that I can improve my drawing skills
that give me hope to create a masterpiece in the future.
